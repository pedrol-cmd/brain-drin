# Content Repurposing Engine

## Purpose
To maximize the value of every research or creative effort by reaching audiences on multiple platforms.

## Transform Matrix
- **Core (e.g., Whitepaper) → 5 LinkedIn Posts**.
- **Core → 1 Thread on X**.
- **Core → Newsletter Summary**.
- **Core → 3 Short Video Scripts (Reels/TikTok)**.
- **Core → FAQ / Help Center update**.

## Rules
- Don't just copy-paste; adapt the *Tone* and *Hook* to the platform.
- Every piece of repurposed content must link back to the "Core."
