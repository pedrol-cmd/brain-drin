# Customer Journey Mapping

## Purpose
To visualize the user's emotional and behavioral path, identifying moments of delight and friction.

## Journey Phases
1. **Awareness**: First touch (Ad, Social, Search).
2. **Consideration**: Comparing, reading docs, watching demo.
3. **Activation**: Sign up and "Aha!" moment.
4. **Retention**: Regular use and value realization.
5. **Advocacy**: Referral and community participation.

## Output
A `JOURNEY-MAP.md` using Mermaid `journey` diagrams.
