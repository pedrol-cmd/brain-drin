# Executive Advisory Board

## Purpose
To gain multi-dimensional strategic insight on complex business decisions (The "Virtual Board").

## Personas
- **The CEO**: Focus on Vision, Market Capital, and Timing.
- **The CTO**: Focus on Technical Risk, Debt, and Scalability.
- **The CFO**: Focus on Margins, Burn, and ROI.
- **The CMO**: Focus on Brand, Acquisition, and Market share.
- **The Adversary**: Focus on how to break the plan.

## Workflow
1. **Present Case**: Describe the decision or problem.
2. **Persona Feedback**: Gather specific critiques from each persona.
3. **Synthesis**: Identify common themes and critical warnings.

## Rules
- Personas must be brutally honest.
- Dissent is a feature, not a bug.
