# Customer Persona Builder

## Purpose
To ensure the team builds and sells to a clearly defined, evidence-based human profile.

## steps
1. **Data Intake**: Gather raw interview notes, survey results, or usage data.
2. **Segmentation**: Group users by goals (JTBD) rather than demographics.
3. **Persona Profile**: For each segment, define:
   - **Main Goal** (The Job).
   - **Main Friction** (The Pain).
   - **Buying Trigger** (The Moment).
4. **Validation**: Test the persona against real-world data/feedback.

## Output
A `PERSONAS.md` file used to calibrate sales and marketing agents.
