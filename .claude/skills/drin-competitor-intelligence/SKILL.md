# Competitor Intelligence

## Purpose
To maintain a real-time understanding of the competitive landscape, identifying gaps and opportunities for differentiation.

## Workflow
1. **Identify**: List top 3-5 direct and indirect competitors.
2. **Scrape & Analyze**: Use `@researcher` to gather pricing, features, and public reviews.
3. **SWOT Analysis**: Map their strengths and weaknesses against our product.
4. **Differentiation Strategy**: Identify the "Unfair Advantage" or Blue Ocean space.

## Output
A `COMPETITIVE-LANDSCAPE.md` report with actionable strategic moves.
