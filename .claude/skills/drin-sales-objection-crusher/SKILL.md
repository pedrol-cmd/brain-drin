# Sales Objection Crusher

## Purpose
To increase conversion rates by preparing the sales team (human or AI) for any pushback.

## The Matrix
For every objection (e.g., "Too expensive," "Already using competitor"), provide:
1. **Acknowledge**: Empathic validation.
2. **Reframe**: Shifting perspective.
3. **Evidence**: Fact or case study.
4. **The Turn**: Closing question or bridge back to value.

## Output
A `SALES-PLAYBOOK.md` that is updated after every lost deal.
