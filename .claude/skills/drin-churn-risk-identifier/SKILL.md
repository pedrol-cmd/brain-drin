# Churn Risk Identifier

## Purpose
To protect revenue by identifying users who are falling out of love with the product before they leave.

## Risk Signals
- **Usage Drop**: Sudden decrease in login frequency or session duration.
- **Feature Abandonment**: Stopping the use of a core sticky feature.
- **Support Volume**: Sudden spike (frustration) or sudden silence (apathy).
- **Payment Failures**: Leading indicator of credit card rot or intentional non-payment.

## Actions
1. **Alert**: Automated notification for CSMs.
2. **Outreach**: Personalized "Value-recap" email.
3. **Survey**: Identifying the specific pain point.
4. **Rescue**: Retaining through discounts, training, or bug fixes.
