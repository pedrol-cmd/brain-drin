# Market Entry Playbook

## Purpose
To reduce the risk of expansion by following a methodical, data-backed entry strategy.

## Phases
1. **Audit**: Regulation, local competition, and cultural nuances.
2. **Localization**: Adjusting Product, Pricing, and Brand voice.
3. **Soft Launch**: Small, targeted test to validate assumptions.
4. **Blitz**: Full-force acquisition and awareness campaign.

## Output
A `MARKET-ENTRY-PLAN.md` with localized KPIs.
