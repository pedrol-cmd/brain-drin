# WhatsApp Outbound Engine

## Purpose
To leverage the high open rates of WhatsApp while maintaining professional boundaries and trust.

## Guidelines
- **Speed**: Respond in < 5 mins if possible.
- **Micro-copy**: Messages under 3 lines.
- **Media**: Use voice notes or Loom links for high-touch leads.
- **Timing**: Respect timezone and business hour courtesy.

## Steps
1. **Context Check**: Why are we messaging now? (Signal).
2. **Intimacy Level**: Warm vs. Cold approach.
3. **CTA**: Focus on a specific next step (Book a call, View doc).
