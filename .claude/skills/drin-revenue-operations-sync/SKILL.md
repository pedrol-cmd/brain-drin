# Revenue Operations (RevOps) Sync

## Purpose
To eliminate silos and ensure that every customer touchpoint is reflected in the revenue data.

## Sync Points
- **MQL to SQL Handover**: Automation of lead qualification and routing.
- **Churn to Product Feedback**: Ensuring lost deals inform the roadmap.
- **Upsell Identification**: Triggering success outreach based on high usage.
- **Unified Reporting**: One source of truth for the entire funnel.

## Rules
- Data hygiene is the #1 priority.
- Standardize terminology across departments (What is a "Leads"?).
