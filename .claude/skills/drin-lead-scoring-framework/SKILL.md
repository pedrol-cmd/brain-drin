# Lead Scoring Framework

## Purpose
To focus sales energy on the leads most likely to convert, maximizing ROI.

## Components
1. **Demographic Fit**: Industry, Role, Company size, Tech stack.
2. **Behavioral Depth**: Specific actions (e.g., Visited pricing, Used feature X, Exported data).
3. **Trigger Signals**: Recent hiring, funding, or executive changes.

## Scoring Model
- 0-30: Cold (Nurture).
- 31-70: Warm (Automated outreach).
- 71-100: Hot (Immediate human sales intervention).
