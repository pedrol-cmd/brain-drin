# Brand Voice Architect

## Purpose
To ensure that every piece of content produced by the team sounds like the brand, protecting authority and unique positioning.

## Step 1: Voice Discovery
Analyze existing high-performing content or user preferences to extract:
- **Tone**: (e.g., Professional, Conversational, Academic, Provocative)
- **Vocabulary**: Words we use vs. words we avoid (Hard-bans).
- **Structure**: Preferred sentence length, level of jargon, and use of rhetoric.

## Step 2: Personality Matrix
Define the brand as a person:
- "We are [X], but we are NOT [Y]."
- Example: "Short and punchy, but not aggressive."

## Step 3: Guidelines Generation
Output a `BRAND-VOICE.md` file that can be fed to other agents.

## Template (Rules)
- Never use AI-clichés (landscape, revolutionize, game-changer).
- Use active voice.
- Focus on the "Unique Insight" for every paragraph.
