# Market Sizing (TAM/SAM/SOM)

## Purpose
To validate business opportunity and provide data for strategy or fund-raising.

## Framework
1. **TAM (Total Addressable Market)**: Everyone who could theoretically use the product.
2. **SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market)**: The portion of TAM that fits our current product/geo/segment.
3. **SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market)**: The portion of SAM we can realistically capture in the next 1-3 years.

## Calculation Methods
- **Bottom-up**: (Number of customers) x (Avg Contract Value). (Preferred).
- **Top-down**: % of a reported industry total.
