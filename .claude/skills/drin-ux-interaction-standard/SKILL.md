# UX Interaction Standard

## Purpose
To provide a cohesive, premium-feel experience through consistent and predictable UI behaviors.

## Standards
- **Wait States**: Skeletons vs. Spinners.
- **Success Feedback**: Toasts vs. Animations.
- **Error Feedback**: Inline vs. Modal vs. Passive.
- **Transitions**: Ease-in/out speeds and durations.

## Rules
- Interaction must happen in < 100ms.
- Success should feel "Delightful"; Errors should feel "Helpful."
