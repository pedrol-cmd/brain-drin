# SEO Content Optimizer

## Purpose
To ensure every piece of text produced has the maximum chance of ranking and attracting organic traffic.

## Audit Checklist
1. **Intent Match**: Does this answer the user's specific query?
2. **Keyword Placement**: Title, H1, First Paragraph, and Naturally throughout.
3. **Readability**: Are sentences short and headers descriptive?
4. **Metadata**: Compelling title tags and meta descriptions.
5. **Links**: Internal links to relevant local content and external links to authority sources.

## Rules
- No keyword stuffing.
- Quality and utility over length.
