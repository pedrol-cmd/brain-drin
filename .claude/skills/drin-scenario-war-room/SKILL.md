# Scenario War Room

## Purpose
To build organizational resilience and rapid-response capabilities for high-uncertainty events.

## Scenarios
- **The Black Swan**: Sudden market crash or competitor move.
- **The Rocketship**: Sudden 10x user growth.
- **The Breach**: Major security incident.
- **The Pivot**: Strategic shift in business model.

## Workflow
1. **Define Scenario**: Describe the event and its probability.
2. **Impact Map**: Who is affected? What is at risk?
3. **Response Options**: List 3 potential strategies (Safe, Aggressive, Balanced).
4. **Action Plan**: Immediate steps if the trigger occurs.

## Rules
- Focus on "Second-Order Effects."
- Identify the "Early Warning Signals" for every scenario.
