# Agile Ceremony Moderator

## Purpose
To keep team ceremonies focused, efficient, and valuable, avoiding "meeting fatigue."

## Daily Standup (15 min)
1. What was done yesterday?
2. What will be done today?
3. Any blockers?

## Sprint Planning
1. Review Sprint Goal.
2. Select stories from prioritized backlog.
3. Validate capacity and velocity.

## Retrospective
1. What went well?
2. What could be improved?
3. Action items for the next sprint.

## Rules
- No deep technical problem solving during the daily (take it to a "parking lot").
- Every voice must be heard in the retro.
