# Investor Pitch Deck Check

## Purpose
To ensure the vision is communicable, the data is solid, and the "Ask" is clear.

## The Checklist
- **The Problem**: Is it painful and undeniable?
- **The Solution**: Is it unique and scalable?
- **The Traction**: Is the data honest and impressive?
- **The Team**: Why are these the right people?
- **The Market**: Is it big enough to matter?
- **The Ask**: What do we need and what will it achieve?

## Rules
- One slide, one idea.
- Focus on the "Story," not just the stats.
- Design must be professional and premium.
