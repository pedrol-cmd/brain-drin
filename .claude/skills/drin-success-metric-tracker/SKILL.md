# Success Metric Tracker

## Purpose
To ensure every feature and initiative is measured against actual business and user value.

## Metrics Categories
1. **North Star**: The single best indicator of value delivery (e.g., Messages sent, Files uploaded).
2. **Leading Indicators**: Predictive of North Star growth.
3. **Lagging Indicators**: Revenue, Churn (Result of past actions).
4. **Counter-metrics**: Ensuring we don't optimize one thing by breaking another (e.g., High conversion vs High refund rate).

## Rules
- Every feature must have a defined metric before launch.
- Review metrics weekly during the growth sync.
