# Growth Experiment Runner

## Purpose
To instill a culture of rapid testing and data-driven learning in the team.

## Workflow
1. **Ideation**: List all growth ideas in a backlog.
2. **ICE Scoring**: Grade by Impact, Confidence, and Ease.
3. **Experiment Design**: Define Hypothesis, Success Metric, and Duration.
4. **Execution Log**: Track the implementation and any deviations.
5. **Conclusion**: Document the result (Win, Loss, Inconclusive) and next steps.

## Template
Use `EXP-[Number]-[Topic].md` for every test.
