# Company Culture Auditor

## Purpose
To build a sustainable, high-performing organization by protecting its most valuable asset: the people.

## Checkpoints
- **Alignment**: Does the team know the Vision?
- **Psychological Safety**: Do people speak up about mistakes?
- **Burnout Heatmap**: Identifying nodes of high stress/hours.
- **Diversity & Inclusion**: Is every voice truly heard and valued?

## Output
A `CULTURE-HEALTH-REPORT.md` with prioritized cultural interventions.
