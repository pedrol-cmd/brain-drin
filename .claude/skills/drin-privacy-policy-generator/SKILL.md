# Privacy Policy Generator

## Purpose
To maintain legally accurate and transparent data privacy documentation as the system evolves.

## Intake
- **Data Categories**: What do we collect?
- **Processing Purpose**: Why do we collect it?
- **Third Parties**: Who do we share it with? (Cloud providers, Analytics, Payment).
- **Retention**: How long do we keep it?

## Rules
- Use plain language alongside legal jargon.
- Must be updated every time a new data processor is added.
