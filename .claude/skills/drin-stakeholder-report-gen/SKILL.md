# Stakeholder Report Generator

## Purpose
To ensure the right information reaches the right people at the right frequency, building trust and transparency.

## Audience Levels
1. **Executive**: Bottom line, North Star metric, blockers, and next big moves (1 min read).
2. **Product/Marketing**: Funnel metrics, experiment results, and roadmap status.
3. **Engineering**: Technical debt, velocity, and infrastructure health.

## Components
- Highlights (Wins).
- Lowlights (Lessons).
- KPIs (The Data).
- Requests (What we need from you).
