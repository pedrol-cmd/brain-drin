# Pricing Strategy Experiment

## Purpose
To ensure that the price reflects the value delivered and maximizes long-term revenue.

## Models to Test
- **Freemium vs. Free Trial**.
- **Seat-based vs. Usage-based**.
- **Tiered Packaging (Good, Better, Best)**.
- **Annual vs. Monthly**.

## Workflow
1. **Hypothesis**: "Increasing Price X will drive Metric Y."
2. **Test Design**: A/B test or Cohort-based release.
3. **Data Collection**: ARPU, LTV, Churn, and Conversion rates.
4. **Analysis**: Was the trade-off worth it?

## Rules
- Never make radical pricing changes for existing users without high-touch comms.
- Price for value, not cost.
